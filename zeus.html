Transliteration

Zeus

Roman Spelling

Jupiter, Jove

Zeus, the youngest child of Cronus and Rhea. Cronus devoured each of his children as they were born, but Rhea saved Zeus by giving him a stone wrapped in swaddling clothes instead and hid him in a cave on Crete, nurtured by the nymph Amalthaea and protected by the Curetes, who clashed their weapons together to muffle his cries. Once grown, Zeus led a revolt against the Titans, dethroning Cronus and dividing the world among his siblings.Zeus was the King of the Gods and the god of the sky, weather, law and order, destiny and fate, and kingship.His usual attributes were a lightning bolt, a royal sceptre and an eagle.
